What we believe

Most booking platforms take 10–30% of every reservation as a commission. For small, independent park operators — many of them family-run businesses — that's money that should stay in their pocket, not flow to a platform.

We built RVSpot on a different model: operators pay a flat monthly fee for Park Pro, and keep 100% of every booking. Travelers get a better experience because parks aren't inflating prices to cover platform fees. Everyone wins.

We also believe that travelers deserve honest information — real Wi-Fi speeds, accurate amenity listings, and reviews from people who actually stayed there. Not curated marketing copy.
