4. Bookings and Payments

When you book a site through RVSpot, you enter into a direct agreement with the park operator. RVSpot facilitates the transaction but is not a party to the rental agreement between you and the park.

Cancellations and refunds are governed by the individual park's cancellation policy, displayed at the time of booking. RVSpot service fees may be non-refundable depending on the cancellation timeline.

Commission (Free-tier parks): For parks on the free listing tier, RVSpot collects a 10% platform fee on each booking transaction and remits the balance to the park operator within 5–7 business days following the guest's check-in date.

Pro Park bookings: Parks on the Pro Park plan handle payments directly. RVSpot facilitates the payment processing via Stripe but does not charge a per-booking commission.

All payments are processed by Stripe. By making a payment, you agree to Stripe's Terms of Service.
