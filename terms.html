7. Cancellations & Refunds

Cancellation policies vary by park. Review the park's cancellation policy before booking.

Each park sets its own cancellation policy. Common policies include:

  • Flexible: Full refund if cancelled 24+ hours before check-in
  • Moderate: Full refund if cancelled 5+ days before check-in
  • Strict: 50% refund if cancelled 7+ days before check-in; no refund thereafter

The 3% RVSpot platform fee is non-refundable in all cases. If a park cancels your confirmed booking, you will receive a full refund including the platform fee.
