Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. You can cancel Nomad Pro or Park Pro at any time from your account dashboard. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period. No cancellation fees.
What is the 3% booking fee?
RVSpot charges a 3% platform service fee on bookings processed through the platform. Park Pro subscribers pay zero commission — the $79/month subscription covers all booking fees.
How do operator payouts work?
Park Pro operators connect a Stripe account and receive payouts 2–7 business days after guest check-in. Stripe's standard processing fees apply (typically 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction).
Is there a free trial for Park Pro?
We don't currently offer a free trial, but your first month is risk-free — if you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.
How do I claim my park listing?
Visit your park's page on RVSpot and click "Claim this park." We'll verify ownership via phone or business email and have your listing active within 24 hours.
